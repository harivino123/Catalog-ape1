teflon tape

PTFE thread seal tape, is a thin, non‑adhesive film used to wrap pipe threads to create a watertight or airtight seal. It reduces friction, fills gaps between threads, and makes fittings easier to tighten and disassemble in plumbing, gas, and air systems.

surface protection tape

Surface protection tape is a temporary adhesive film designed to shield surfaces from scratches, dust, stains, and abrasions during handling, transport, or installation. It adheres securely without leaving residue, ensuring the underlying material remains clean and intact once removed.

Aluminium foil tape is a durable, reflective adhesive tape made with a metallic foil backing, widely used in HVAC, electrical, and construction applications. It provides strong sealing, insulation, and moisture resistance, making it ideal for ductwork, shielding, and bonding tasks.
